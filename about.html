The Human Behind the Systems

AI consultant. Travel professional. Wellness advocate. Building systems that prioritize the human element.

The Journey

From production floors to digital architectures.

I'm Peggens Altidor, a Haitian American entrepreneur and AI architect. My story is one of constant evolution -- from working in production and hospitality, where I learned the irreplaceable value of precision, service, and reading the room, to building AI-powered systems that carry those same values into the digital world.

My background in hospitality taught me something that no coding bootcamp ever could: technology only matters when it serves people well. That principle shapes everything I build -- from AI agents with genuine personality to travel experiences with cultural depth to wellness programs rooted in ancestral wisdom.

Today, I operate under Altidor Wellness LLC, with flywithpeggs as my consumer-facing brand. I bring together three disciplines -- AI consulting, travel planning, and wellness coaching -- under one philosophy: precision, empathy, and story.

My Philosophy

Three pillars that guide every project, every conversation, every journey.

Precision

From production, I learned that details matter. Every system I build is technically robust, tested, and designed to perform -- not just look good on a slide deck.

Empathy

From hospitality, I learned that people come first. My AI systems aren't cold automations -- they're warm, culturally aware, and built to serve real human needs.

Story

Every journey, every brand, every agent has a narrative. I don't just build systems -- I craft experiences that resonate, connect, and stay with you.
